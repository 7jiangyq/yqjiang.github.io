About Me

I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. at the College of Information Science and Electronic Engineering at Zhejiang University, under the supervision of Prof. Cheng Zhuo, Prof. Xunzhao Yin, and Researcher Qi Sun. Prior to this, I received my Master's degree from Beijing Jiaotong University.

My research interests include large-model-assisted integrated circuit design, reinforcement learning, and multimodal large-model reasoning.
